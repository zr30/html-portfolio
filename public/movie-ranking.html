The Best Movies According to Zach

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Everything Everywhere All At Once

This film was as a gorgeous as it was evocative. It effortlessly covered themes spanning from the struggles of mother daughter relationships, the challenges of immigrants, existentialism, what it means to grow up in a maximalist world made possible by the internet and social media. I felt heard in some regards but mostly was just in awe.

Into the Wild

There is something so romantic about leaving society behind and returning to nature. Giving up all material possessions for a journey inward and trying to find sense in the world. I resonate with a lot of sentiments of the main character

Pitch Perfect

This movie is just so entertaining and always makes me smile
